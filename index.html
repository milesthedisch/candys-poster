Come to this workshop if you want to understand how to use local clay soil to build strong cob walls.

Workshop Covers: 

  • Understanding all of the ingredients in cob
  • How to test soil for clay content & establish a recipe
  • Low-tech mixing techniques
  • How to attach cob to framing elements
  • Tons of hands-on building experience with one-on-one guidance
